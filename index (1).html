About the Author

Pedro Melody is a passionate author whose works center around faith, communication, and building strong, healthy relationships. Through books like the "Mutual Communication Healthy Relationship" series and "Faith in Marriage," he helps individuals find clarity and God's wisdom for their personal challenges. He's also an Evangelist who has traveled the world proclaiming the Gospel, in addition to his duties He's the founder/president of singforGodacademy(SFGA) he's an anointed and favorites guests speakers at many churches concerts conferences conventions and seminars across west Africa. He's a young man who's filled and operates in the power of the Holy Spirit. A devoted servant of God whose passionate mission and goals is to spread the gosple of christ to the ends of the earth.
